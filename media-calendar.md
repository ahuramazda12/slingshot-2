# Slingshot 2.0 Media Calendar

## Weekly Programming Schedule

| Day | Time (UTC) | Segment | Type | Monetization |
|-----|------------|---------|------|--------------|
| **Tuesday** | 4:00 PM | Slingshot Alpha | Market news, trending narratives | Sponsor intro slot ($500) |
| **Thursday** | 4:00 PM | Project Spotlight | Partner interview | Partner pays (Space Spotlight $3K) |
| **Saturday** | 6:00 PM | Degen Hours | Community vibes, games, giveaways | Giveaway sponsors |

---

## Segment Details

### Tuesday: Slingshot Alpha (4PM UTC)

**Format:** 45-60 minutes
**Hosts:** 2 Squadron members (rotating)
**Audience:** Existing community + crypto-curious

**Content Structure:**
1. **Opening** (5 min) - Sponsor shoutout if applicable
2. **Market Recap** (10 min) - What happened this week
3. **Alpha Segment** (15 min) - Trending narratives, opportunities
4. **Community Questions** (10 min) - Pull from audience
5. **What We're Watching** (5 min) - Each host shares one thing
6. **Close** (5 min) - Recap, next Space promo

**Monetization:**
- Sponsor intro slot: $500
- "This episode of Slingshot Alpha is brought to you by [Sponsor]"
- 60-second sponsor mention at opening
- Sponsor pinned in Space

**Prep Required:**
- 30 min research before Space
- 3-5 talking points prepared
- 1 sponsor script if applicable

---

### Thursday: Project Spotlight (4PM UTC)

**Format:** 60 minutes
**Hosts:** 2 Squadron members + Partner guest(s)
**Audience:** Partner's community + Slingshot community

**Content Structure:**
1. **Opening** (5 min) - Introduce Slingshot, introduce guest
2. **Project Overview** (10 min) - What is it, why does it matter
3. **Deep Dive** (20 min) - Technical, roadmap, differentiators
4. **Team Background** (10 min) - Who's building, why them
5. **Community Q&A** (10 min) - Live questions from audience
6. **Alpha/Announcements** (5 min) - Anything exclusive for listeners

**Monetization:**
- This IS the paid product (Space Spotlight package: $3,000)
- Partner pays for the slot
- If no paying partner: Feature promising project for relationship building

**Prep Required:**
- Research project thoroughly (1 hour)
- Prepare 10 questions (use 5-7)
- Coordinate with partner on announcements
- Confirm any alpha they want to drop

---

### Saturday: Degen Hours (6PM UTC)

**Format:** 60-90 minutes (loose)
**Hosts:** 2-3 Squadron members
**Audience:** Core community, degens, weekend crowd

**Content Structure:**
1. **Vibes Check** (10 min) - How's everyone doing, week recap
2. **Degen Picks** (15 min) - Each host shares something spicy (not financial advice)
3. **Games/Trivia** (20 min) - Crypto trivia, predictions, debates
4. **Giveaway** (10 min) - If sponsored
5. **Open Mic** (15+ min) - Community members come up
6. **Raid Call** (5 min) - Coordinate engagement on something

**Monetization:**
- Giveaway sponsors provide prizes + $500 for promotion
- "Tonight's giveaway brought to you by [Sponsor]"
- Lower pressure, relationship building focus

**Prep Required:**
- Minimal - this is meant to be casual
- Have trivia questions ready
- Coordinate giveaway if applicable

- [ ] Post key moments thread within 2 hours
- [ ] Share recording link
- [ ] Thank guest publicly
- [ ] Log metrics in tracking sheet
- [ ] Send partner report (if paid)

## Host Rotation

To prevent burnout and build depth:

| Week | Tuesday (Alpha) | Thursday (Spotlight) | Saturday (Degen) |
|------|-----------------|---------------------|------------------|
| 1 | Host A + B | Host C + D | Host E + F |
| 2 | Host C + D | Host E + F | Host A + B |
| 3 | Host E + F | Host A + B | Host C + D |
| 4 | Host A + B | Host C + D | Host E + F |

- Everyone hosts ~1.3 Spaces per week
- Pairs rotate to build chemistry variety
- Spaces Lead coordinates schedule

## Booking Calendar

### How Partners Book Thursday Spotlight
1. Partner purchases Space Spotlight package ($3,000)
2. Spaces Lead sends available dates (next 2 weeks minimum)
3. Partner confirms date
4. Spaces Lead adds to calendar, begins coordination
5. Guest prep call scheduled (optional but recommended)

### Availability Rules
- Thursday slots: 2 available per month for paid partners
- If more demand: Waitlist or schedule Tuesday Alpha takeover ($2,000)
- Holiday weeks: Announce schedule changes 1 week ahead

## Emergency Protocols

### Guest No-Show
1. Wait 10 minutes
2. If no response: Convert to community AMA or market discussion
3. Apologize to audience, don't throw guest under bus
4. Follow up with guest privately after
5. Reschedule or refund based on circumstances

### Technical Issues
1. If host can't access: Backup host takes over
2. If Space crashes: Restart immediately, tweet new link
3. If recording fails: Apologize, offer partial refund or bonus content

### Low Attendance
1. Don't panic - quality over quantity
2. Engage deeply with who's there
3. Create great content anyway (recording matters)
4. Review promo strategy after
