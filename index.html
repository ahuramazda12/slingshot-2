4-Week Outreach Cycle

1

Research

Each Squadron member identifies 10 targets. Criteria: Recently funded, launching soon, active Twitter.

2

Outreach

Send personalized DMs. 50+ per week across Squadron. No copy-paste spam.

3

Qualify & Close

15-min discovery call. Send proposal. Close within 72 hours or move on.

4

Execute & Report

Deliver campaign. Send metrics report. Collect testimonial. Upsell.

Discovery Call Questions (15 min)

  • "What's your biggest marketing challenge right now?"
  • "What's your timeline? Anything launching soon?"
  • "Have you worked with KOLs before? How did that go?"
  • "What does success look like in the next 30-60 days?"
  • "Do you have budget allocated for marketing support?"
