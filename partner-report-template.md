# Metrics Collection Guide

## How to Gather Metrics

### Twitter Analytics (for threads)
1. Click on tweet
2. Click "View analytics"
3. Screenshot impressions, engagements, detail expands

### Space Metrics
1. During Space: Screenshot listener count at peak
2. After Space: Twitter sends email with stats (check host's email)
3. Recording views: Check 48 hours after posting

### Engagement Raid
1. Screenshot target tweet BEFORE raid starts (note current engagement)
2. Screenshot AFTER raid window closes
3. Calculate delta

### Follower Count
1. Check partner's profile BEFORE campaign
2. Check 48-72 hours AFTER campaign
3. Note: Many factors affect followers, present as correlation not causation

## Tools

- **Screenshots:** Native OS screenshot or Lightshot
- **Thread analytics:** Twitter native or Tweet Hunter (if available)
- **Tracking:** Log everything in shared Google Sheet or Notion
